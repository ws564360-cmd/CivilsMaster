🌾 Agriculture Economy (వ్యవసాయ ఆర్థిక వ్యవస్థ)

Agriculture is an important sector of the Indian economy providing employment and supporting rural development.

తెలుగులో: భారత ఆర్థిక వ్యవస్థలో వ్యవసాయం ముఖ్యమైన రంగం. ఇది ఉపాధి మరియు గ్రామీణ అభివృద్ధికి సహాయపడుతుంది.

1. Role of Agriculture in India

  • Provides employment to a large population.
  • Provides raw materials for industries.
  • Supports food security.
  • Contributes to rural economy.

2. Types of Farming

  • Subsistence Farming: Farming mainly for self-consumption.
  • Commercial Farming: Farming for market sale.
  • Mixed Farming: Combination of crops and livestock.

3. Cropping Patterns in India

  • Kharif Crops - Sown with monsoon.
  • Rabi Crops - Grown in winter season.
  • Zaid Crops - Short duration summer crops.

4. Major Crops of India

Rice

Important food crop requiring high temperature and rainfall.

Wheat

Major rabi crop grown in northern India.

Cotton

Important cash crop and raw material for textile industry.

Sugarcane

Important commercial crop used for sugar production.

5. Green Revolution

Green Revolution increased agricultural production through high yielding variety seeds, fertilizers and irrigation.

  • Started in India during the 1960s.
  • Associated with increased wheat and rice production.
  • Dr. M.S. Swaminathan played an important role.

6. Irrigation in India

  • Canals
  • Wells
  • Tube Wells
  • Dams

Irrigation reduces dependence on rainfall.

7. Minimum Support Price (MSP)

MSP is the minimum price announced by the government to protect farmers from price fluctuations.

తెలుగులో: రైతులకు ధరల రక్షణ కల్పించడానికి ప్రభుత్వం కనీస మద్దతు ధరను ప్రకటిస్తుంది.

8. Agricultural Challenges

  • Small land holdings
  • Dependence on monsoon
  • Low productivity
  • Storage problems
  • Market challenges

UPSC Important Points 🎯

  • Agriculture supports rural economy.
  • Green Revolution improved food grain production.
  • MSP protects farmers from price fall.
  • Kharif crops depend mainly on monsoon.
  • Irrigation improves agricultural productivity.
