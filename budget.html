💰 Union Budget & Fiscal Policy (కేంద్ర బడ్జెట్ మరియు ద్రవ్య విధానం)

The Union Budget is the annual financial statement of the Government of India showing estimated receipts and expenditure.

తెలుగులో: కేంద్ర బడ్జెట్ అనేది ఒక సంవత్సరానికి ప్రభుత్వ ఆదాయం మరియు ఖర్చుల వివరాలను చూపించే ఆర్థిక ప్రకటన.

1. Constitutional Provision

  • Article 112 deals with Annual Financial Statement.
  • Presented by the Finance Minister in Parliament.
  • Approved by Parliament before implementation.

2. Types of Budget Receipts

Revenue Receipts

  • Tax Revenue
  • Non-Tax Revenue

Revenue receipts do not create liabilities for the government.

Capital Receipts

  • Borrowings
  • Recovery of Loans
  • Disinvestment

3. Government Expenditure

Revenue Expenditure

Expenditure on salaries, subsidies, interest payments and regular activities.

Capital Expenditure

Expenditure that creates assets like infrastructure and development projects.

4. Fiscal Deficit (ద్రవ్య లోటు)

Fiscal deficit is the difference between total government expenditure and total receipts excluding borrowings.

తెలుగులో: ప్రభుత్వ మొత్తం ఖర్చులు, రుణాలు కాకుండా వచ్చే ఆదాయం కంటే ఎక్కువగా ఉన్నప్పుడు ద్రవ్య లోటు ఏర్పడుతుంది.

5. Revenue Deficit

Revenue deficit occurs when revenue expenditure is higher than revenue receipts.

6. Primary Deficit

Primary deficit is fiscal deficit minus interest payments.

7. Fiscal Policy

Fiscal policy refers to government decisions regarding taxation and expenditure to manage the economy.

  • Tax Policy
  • Government Spending
  • Public Debt Management

8. FRBM Act

Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management Act aims to maintain fiscal discipline and reduce excessive deficits.

తెలుగులో: ప్రభుత్వ ఆర్థిక క్రమశిక్షణను పెంచడానికి FRBM చట్టం తీసుకువచ్చారు.

UPSC Important Points 🎯

  • Article 112 deals with Union Budget.
  • Budget is presented by Finance Minister.
  • Fiscal deficit indicates government borrowing requirement.
  • Revenue expenditure does not create assets.
  • Capital expenditure creates assets.
