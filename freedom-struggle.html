🇮🇳 Indian National Movement (స్వాతంత్ర్య ఉద్యమం)

The Indian National Movement was a long struggle against British colonial rule that resulted in India's independence in 1947.

తెలుగులో: భారత జాతీయ ఉద్యమం బ్రిటిష్ పాలనకు వ్యతిరేకంగా జరిగిన దీర్ఘకాల పోరాటం. దీని ఫలితంగా 1947లో భారతదేశం స్వాతంత్ర్యం పొందింది.

1. Formation of Indian National Congress (1885)

  • Founded in 1885.
  • Founder: A.O. Hume.
  • First President: W.C. Bonnerjee.
  • First session was held at Bombay.

2. Moderates and Extremists

Moderates (1885-1905)

They believed in constitutional methods and peaceful reforms.

  • Dadabhai Naoroji
  • Gopal Krishna Gokhale
  • Surendranath Banerjee

Extremists

They demanded stronger action against British rule.

  • Bal Gangadhar Tilak
  • Lala Lajpat Rai
  • Bipin Chandra Pal

3. Swadeshi Movement (1905)

The Swadeshi Movement started after the partition of Bengal in 1905.

తెలుగులో: 1905 బెంగాల్ విభజనకు వ్యతిరేకంగా స్వదేశీ ఉద్యమం ప్రారంభమైంది.

4. Gandhian Era

Champaran Satyagraha (1917)

Gandhi's first Satyagraha movement in India.

Non-Cooperation Movement (1920)

Started against British policies after Jallianwala Bagh incident.

Civil Disobedience Movement (1930)

Started with the Salt March (Dandi March).

Quit India Movement (1942)

Launched with the slogan "Do or Die".

5. Revolutionary Movement

  • Bhagat Singh
  • Chandra Shekhar Azad
  • Subhas Chandra Bose

6. Independence of India (1947)

  • India became independent on 15 August 1947.
  • British rule ended after a long freedom struggle.
  • India was partitioned into India and Pakistan.

UPSC Important Points 🎯

  • INC was founded in 1885.
  • Partition of Bengal happened in 1905.
  • Non-Cooperation Movement started in 1920.
  • Dandi March happened in 1930.
  • Quit India Movement started in 1942.
  • India got independence in 1947.
