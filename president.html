🇮🇳 President of India (రాష్ట్రపతి)

The President of India is the constitutional head and the first citizen of India. The President represents the unity and integrity of the nation.

తెలుగులో: భారత రాష్ట్రపతి దేశానికి రాజ్యాంగ అధిపతి మరియు మొదటి పౌరుడు. రాష్ట్రపతి దేశ ఐక్యత మరియు సమగ్రతకు ప్రతీక.

Constitutional Provisions

  • Article 52 - There shall be a President of India.
  • Articles 52 to 62 deal with the President.
  • Part V of the Constitution deals with Union Executive.

Election of President

The President is elected indirectly by an Electoral College.

Electoral College includes:

  • Elected members of Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha.
  • Elected members of State Legislative Assemblies.
  • Elected members of Legislative Assemblies of Delhi and Puducherry.

తెలుగులో: రాష్ట్రపతిని ప్రజలు నేరుగా ఎన్నుకోరు. ఎన్నికల కళాశాల (Electoral College) ద్వారా ఎన్నుకుంటారు.

Qualifications of President

  • Must be a citizen of India.
  • Must have completed 35 years of age.
  • Must be qualified for election as a Lok Sabha member.
  • Must not hold an office of profit.

Term of Office

  • Term: 5 years
  • Eligible for re-election.
  • Resignation is given to the Vice-President.

Powers and Functions

1. Executive Powers

The President appoints the Prime Minister, Governors, Judges of Supreme Court and High Courts.

2. Legislative Powers

The President summons and prorogues Parliament sessions and gives assent to bills.

3. Financial Powers

Money Bills can be introduced in Lok Sabha only with the recommendation of the President.

4. Judicial Powers

The President has the power to grant pardon, reprieve, respite, remission and commutation under Article 72.

5. Emergency Powers

  • Article 352 - National Emergency
  • Article 356 - President's Rule
  • Article 360 - Financial Emergency

Veto Powers

  • Absolute Veto
  • Suspensive Veto
  • Pocket Veto

Impeachment of President

The President can be removed from office for violation of the Constitution under Article 61.

తెలుగులో: రాజ్యాంగ ఉల్లంఘన కారణంగా ఆర్టికల్ 61 ప్రకారం రాష్ట్రపతిపై అభిశంసన (Impeachment) ప్రక్రియ ఉంటుంది.

UPSC Important Points 🎯

  • President is the constitutional head of India.
  • Article 52 creates the office of President.
  • Article 72 deals with pardon power.
  • Article 61 deals with impeachment.
  • President is elected by proportional representation system.
