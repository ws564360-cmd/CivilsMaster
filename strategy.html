English:
A proper strategy is the key to success in the UPSC Civil Services Examination. Consistency, revision and answer writing are more important than studying for long hours.

UPSC సివిల్ సర్వీసెస్ పరీక్షలో విజయం సాధించాలంటే సరైన ప్రణాళిక, నిరంతర సాధన, పునశ్చరణ మరియు Answer Writing చాలా ముఖ్యమైనవి.

📅 12-Month UPSC Study Plan

  • Month 1–2: NCERT Books (Class 6–12)
  • Month 3–4: Standard Books (Laxmikanth, Spectrum, GC Leong, Ramesh Singh)
  • Month 5–6: Complete Optional Subject
  • Month 7–8: Current Affairs + Revision + PYQs
  • Month 9–10: Mock Tests + CSAT Practice
  • Month 11–12: Full Revision + Mains Answer Writing

📚 Daily Study Plan

  • 🌅 Newspaper Reading – 1 Hour
  • 📘 Static Subject – 3 Hours
  • 📝 Answer Writing – 1 Hour
  • 📰 Current Affairs – 1 Hour
  • 📖 Revision – 2 Hours
  • 🎯 MCQ Practice – 1 Hour

📖 Subject-wise Priority

  1. 🏛️ Indian Polity
  2. 🌍 Geography
  3. 📜 History
  4. 💰 Economy
  5. 🌱 Environment
  6. 🔬 Science & Technology
  7. 🎨 Art & Culture
  8. 🌍 International Relations
  9. 📰 Current Affairs

🎯 UPSC Prelims Strategy

  • 📚 Complete NCERTs (Class 6–12).
  • 📖 Read Standard Books thoroughly.
  • 📰 Read Daily Current Affairs.
  • 📝 Solve Previous Year Questions (PYQs).
  • 🎯 Attempt Mock Tests every week.
  • ♻️ Revise multiple times before the exam.

📝 UPSC Mains Strategy

  • ✍️ Practice Answer Writing daily.
  • 📖 Focus on Conceptual Understanding.
  • 📊 Use Flowcharts, Diagrams & Maps.
  • 📰 Integrate Current Affairs with Static Subjects.
  • 📚 Revise Notes regularly.

🎤 UPSC Interview Strategy

  • 😊 Be Honest and Confident.
  • 🗣️ Improve Communication Skills.
