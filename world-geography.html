🌍 World Geography (ప్రపంచ భూగోళ శాస్త్రం)

World Geography studies continents, oceans, landforms, climate, resources and human activities across the world.

తెలుగులో: ప్రపంచ భూగోళ శాస్త్రంలో ఖండాలు, సముద్రాలు, భూ ఆకృతులు, వాతావరణం మరియు వనరులను అధ్యయనం చేస్తారు.

1. Continents of the World (ఖండాలు)

  • Asia
  • Africa
  • North America
  • South America
  • Europe
  • Australia
  • Antarctica

Asia is the largest continent and Antarctica is the coldest continent.

2. Oceans of the World (సముద్రాలు)

  • Pacific Ocean - Largest Ocean
  • Atlantic Ocean
  • Indian Ocean
  • Arctic Ocean
  • Southern Ocean

3. Latitudes and Longitudes

  • Equator divides Earth into Northern and Southern Hemispheres.
  • Prime Meridian passes through Greenwich.
  • Latitudes measure distance north or south of Equator.
  • Longitudes help calculate time.

4. Major Landforms

  • Mountains
  • Plateaus
  • Plains
  • Deserts
  • Valleys

5. Major Mountain Ranges

  • Himalayas - Asia
  • Andes - South America
  • Rocky Mountains - North America
  • Alps - Europe

6. Climate Zones

  • Tropical Climate
  • Temperate Climate
  • Polar Climate

Climate affects agriculture, lifestyle and natural vegetation.

7. Important World Rivers

  • Nile - Africa
  • Amazon - South America
  • Mississippi - North America
  • Yangtze - China
  • Ganga-Brahmaputra - South Asia

8. Natural Resources

  • Coal
  • Petroleum
  • Natural Gas
  • Minerals
  • Forests

UPSC Important Points 🎯

  • Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean.
  • Asia is the largest continent.
  • Equator is at 0° latitude.
  • Prime Meridian is at 0° longitude.
  • Amazon is one of the world's largest rivers by discharge.
